Common Questions

What is Candibly and how does it work?
+

Candibly is an AI receptionist built for US home service businesses. It answers inbound calls, captures customer details, qualifies service needs, and books appointments directly into your workflow — 24/7 with no human required.

Do I need any technical experience to get started?
+

None whatsoever. We handle all setup, phone routing, and integrations. You provide your business info and we get the AI answering calls — usually within 24–48 hours.

What types of calls can Candibly handle?
+

New estimates, emergency requests, appointment scheduling, rescheduling, call routing, and message capture — for HVAC, plumbing, electrical, garage door, and any other home service trade.

Does Candibly offer a free trial?
+

We don't offer a free trial, but we do offer a free demo so you can hear real call flows and see exactly how Candibly fits your workflow before committing.

Which business systems does Candibly integrate with?
+

CRMs like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Pipedrive. Calendars like Google Calendar, Outlook, and Calendly. Field service tools like ServiceTitan and Jobber. Communication tools like Slack and Teams.

What happens when I go over my included minutes?
+

Overage minutes are billed at $0.35/min. We notify you before you hit your limit so there are no surprises, and you can upgrade your plan anytime.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract?
+

No setup fee on Silver. No contracts on any plan — everything is month-to-month. Cancel anytime and your service stays active through the end of the billing period.
