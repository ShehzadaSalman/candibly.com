Privacy Policy

Rajul — Last updated: April 23, 2026

Rajul is a daily self-development app for Muslim men. This policy explains exactly what data the app uses, where it stays, and what it never does.

The short version

All of your data stays on your device. Rajul has no accounts, no backend servers, and no analytics. We never see, collect, or transmit any information you enter into the app — with one narrow exception described below.

What is stored on your device

The following information is saved locally using your device's secure storage:

  • Your display name
  • Your chosen focus pillar (Quwwah, Amanah, Sabr, or Muruwwah)
  • Your daily reminder time preference
  • Lesson completion records (lesson ID, date, pillar)
  • Reflection journal entries (date, prompt, mood, your written answer)
  • Bookmarked lessons
  • App join date
  • Theme and notification preferences
  • Your Claude API key, if you choose to enter one (see below)

None of this data is transmitted off your device. It is never shared with Rajul, third parties, or advertisers.

Local notifications

Rajul can send you a daily reminder notification at the time you choose. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification server is involved and no device token or identifier is sent anywhere.

Optional Claude AI integration

The app includes an optional AI reflection feature. To use it, you enter your own Anthropic Claude API key in Settings. When you submit a reflection prompt:

  • Your prompt text and written answer are sent directly to Anthropic's API using your key.
  • Rajul does not proxy, log, or store this exchange on any server.
  • Your API key is stored only on your device and is never sent to Rajul.
  • Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API requests. See anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

This feature is entirely optional. Rajul works fully without it.

Children

Rajul is designed for adult Muslim men. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 13.

Deleting your data

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from Settings → Reset all data. This permanently removes your name, lesson history, journal entries, bookmarks, and all preferences from the device.

Third-party SDKs

Rajul uses the following Expo / React Native libraries on-device. None of them transmit personal data:

  • expo-secure-store / react-native-mmkv — local encrypted storage
  • expo-notifications — local scheduled notifications only
  • expo-router, react-native-reanimated — navigation and animation

Changes to this policy

If the data practices ever change, this page will be updated with a new effective date. Significant changes will be noted inside the app.
