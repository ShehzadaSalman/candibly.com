Frequently Asked Questions

Candibly FAQs

What is Candibly and how does it work?
+

Candibly is an AI receptionist platform built for US home service companies. It answers inbound calls, captures customer details, qualifies service needs, and books appointments directly into your workflow.

Do I need any technical experience to use the AI answering service?
+

No technical experience required! Our AI receptionist service is designed for easy setup in just minutes. We handle all the technical configuration, phone number routing, and system integrations. Simply provide your business information, and our AI will start answering calls professionally right away.

What types of calls can your virtual receptionist handle?
+

Candibly handles new estimates, emergency requests, appointment scheduling, rescheduling, call routing, and message capture for HVAC, plumbing, electrical, garage door, and related service businesses.

Does Candibly offer a free trial?
+

We do not currently offer a free trial. Schedule a demo to hear real call flows and see how Candibly fits your current process.

Which business systems does the AI receptionist integrate with?
+

Candibly integrates with popular CRM platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive), calendar systems (Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly), and communication tools (Slack, Teams). We also connect with most phone systems and business management platforms for seamless workflow automation.

How much does Candibly cost?
+

Candibly offers three tiers: Silver at $180/month for 500 minutes, Gold at $450/month for 1,200 minutes, and Custom pricing for multi-location or high-volume operators. All plans include 24/7 AI call handling, lead capture, scheduling, and support.

What happens after included minutes?
+

Overage minutes are billed at $0.35/min. We notify you before you hit your limit, and you can upgrade anytime to lock in a better rate.

What counts as a minute?
+

Minutes are the time our AI is actively handling a call, measured by the second from connection to hang-up. Transfers and voicemail handling count only while the AI is engaged.

Is there a setup fee?
+

Standard plans include a $200 one-time setup fee. Complex custom integrations may include additional setup costs.

Do you require a contract?
+

No. All plans are month-to-month, so you can scale up or down as your call volume changes.

What are the cancellation terms?
+

Cancel anytime. Your service stays active through the end of your billing period, and we can export call logs and transcripts on request.
